## **Craft a Professional, Customized Resume with Ease**

Easily craft a professional, customized resume tailored to your needs with the Resume Generator! This user-friendly app gives you full control over your resume’s content, ensuring it’s perfectly suited for any job application.

With the Resume Generator, you can:

- **Customize Every Section:** Dynamically choose which skills, experiences, certifications, and projects to include. Toggle entire sections on or off with a single click.
- **Edit with Precision:** Utilize our enhanced structured editors for Education and Professional Experience—reorder entries using intuitive drag-and-drop, edit multi-field items (like dates and details), and update your information anytime using the advanced JSON editor.
- **Tailor Your Output:** Once all your relevant information is saved, you can easily select exactly which items to include in each resume with just the click of a button. This means you can generate a personalized resume for every role—highlighting the skills and experiences that matter most, and leaving out the irrelevant details.
- **Generate Polished Resumes:** Produce a professional Word document (.docx) with refined formatting, including headers, bullet points, and neatly indented details.
- **Error-Free Customization:** By saving your information upfront, you can create custom resumes with ease and without worrying about errors or typos. The streamlined process minimizes hassle, so you can focus on tailoring your resume to match each job’s requirements.

**Cross-Platform & Hassle-Free:**  
Download the app for your platform—Windows users can grab the [ResumeBuilder.exe](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/dglass710/resume-generator/main/Executable/ResumeBuilder.exe), and Mac users can download the [ResumeBuilder.zip](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/dglass710/resume-generator/main/Executable/ResumeBuilder.zip). No additional software or installations are required; simply download, run, and start creating your perfect resume.
