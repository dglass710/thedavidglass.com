## Build a Custom Resume for Every Application

The Resume Generator lets you save all your skills, experience, certifications, and projects in one place, then pick exactly which items to include for each job application. No retyping, no copy-paste errors.

What it does:

- **Toggle sections on and off** — choose which skills, experiences, and projects appear in each resume with a single click.
- **Structured editing** — drag-and-drop reordering for Education and Professional Experience, multi-field editing for dates and details, and a JSON editor for power users.
- **Tailored output** — save your info once, then generate a different resume for every role by selecting what's relevant.
- **Clean Word documents** — outputs a formatted .docx with headers, bullet points, and proper indentation.
